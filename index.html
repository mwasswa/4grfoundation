4GR Foundation
Vision statement
Helping disabled, orphaned poor and exploited children in particular those in slum areas through providing, promoting, and advancing children’s rights to basic necessities, including education, sports, MDD and healthcare aimed at sustaining their long term development.
more
4GR Foundation
Mission statement
To provide practical support, education, health care and develop talent to improve children quality life and advance their rights.
more
4GR Foundation
main objective
To transform lives of the less priviledged children while availing them platforms to shine and becoming better individuals in their societies

Little About Us
4GR Foundation is an NGO that was formed in 2007 and has continued to grow both in size and strength, with the main goal of giving back to the most vulnerable and less-privileged communities by helping young people in need of assistance to excel and develop their talent in the world of swimming, music, dance and drama, football, chess, volleyball, basketball and tennis ball. The benefits of developing talent in the upbringing of children cannot go un-noticed and therefore 4GR Foundation is committed to talent exploration and skills enhancement for these less-privileged children..
